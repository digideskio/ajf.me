Andrea's website

Hi, I'm Andrea Faulds, a student and an occasional programmer living in Scotland. I currently study German and Language & Linguistics at the University of Aberdeen. I'm best known for contributing to the PHP programming language. You can reach me at ajf@ajf.me.

If you want to read stuff I've written, look at my blog: blog.ajf.me. In October 2015 I gave a talk about PHP 7's Scalar Type Hints at PHP North West!

Some choice things I've made:

  • Warring floodfill cellular automata - Colours that live.
  • win95.ajf.me - A hosted Windows 95 emulator that runs in the browser, powered by Em-DOSBox.
  • PictoSwap - A Nintendo 3DS web app made as a homage to the now-defunct Nintendo Letter Box (aka Swapnote).
  • Crockford Facts.org - A recreation of everyone's favourite Douglas Crockford biographical source.
  • Lots of other things

If you want to keep up with stuff I make, you can follow me on Twitter at @AndreaFaulds. That's just my public account though, it's not very interesting.
